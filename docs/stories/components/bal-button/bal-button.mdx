Buttons are available in four main levels: **primary**, **secondary**, **tertiary**, and **link**. The primary level is specifically designed for prominent call-to-action elements.
Tertiary buttons can be customized with branding colors to align with the overall theme. However, it's important to note that themed tertiary buttons
are not suitable for use on non-white backgrounds.

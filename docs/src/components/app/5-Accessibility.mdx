## WCAG 2.2 AA Compliance

The app component supports patterns that meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA standards:

- **Semantic Landmarks (1.3.1)** — Uses role attributes for app regions
- **Navigable Structure (2.4.1)** — Skip links and landmarks for navigation
- **Keyboard Navigation (2.1.1)** — Tab navigates app and content
- **Focus Management (2.4.3)** — Focus visible and managed appropriately
- **Page Structure (2.4.6)** — Clear visual hierarchy and organization

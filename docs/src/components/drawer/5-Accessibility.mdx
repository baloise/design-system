## WCAG 2.2 AA Compliance

The drawer component implements patterns that meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA standards:

- **Dialog Semantics** — Uses the native `<dialog>` element with `aria-label` for accessible role and naming.
- **Focus Management** — Automatically focuses the drawer content on open and returns focus to the trigger on close.
- **Keyboard Navigation** — Escape key closes the drawer (when `closable` is enabled). Tab order is trapped within the drawer during use.
- **Backdrop Dismiss** — When enabled, clicking outside the panel (on the backdrop) dismisses the drawer, providing a clear visual and tactile way to exit.
- **Contrast & Visibility** — The close button and backdrop have sufficient contrast to be easily visible and distinguished.
